# Numbers & Bases Jokes

1. There are 10 types of people in the world:  
   Those who understand binary, and those who don’t.

2. Why do programmers always mix up Halloween and Christmas?  
   Because Oct 31 = Dec 25.

3. How do programmers count cows?  
   1, 10, 11… (binary counting)

4. Why do hexadecimal numbers make bad roommates?  
   Because they’re always high-fiving (0xF)!
