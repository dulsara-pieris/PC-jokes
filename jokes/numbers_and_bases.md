# Numbers & Bases Jokes

1. Binary is like a language only computers understand; humans just get lost.

2. Why was the computer cold? It left its Windows open.

3. There are only 10 kinds of people: those who understand hexadecimal and those who don’t.

4. Why did the programmer confuse Christmas and Halloween? Because DEC 25 = OCT 31.

5. Why do programmers prefer dark mode? Because light attracts bugs.

6. Why was the function worried about its type? It didn’t want to lose its class.

7. Why are assembly programmers always soaking wet? They work below C-level.

8. Why did the programmer write binary jokes? Because he wanted to be funny in ones and zeros.

9. Why did the decimal cross the road? To get to base 2.

10. There are 10 types of people: those who understand binary and those who don't.

11. There are 10 types of people in the world: Those who understand binary, and those who don’t.

12. Why do programmers always mix up Halloween and Christmas? Because Oct 31 = Dec 25.

13. How do programmers count cows? 1, 10, 11… (binary counting)

14. Why do hexadecimal numbers make bad roommates? Because they’re always high-fiving (0xF)!
